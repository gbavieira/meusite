A little bit about my background

I was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I have moved from Rio when I was still a baby and my endless moving cycle started. Between 1 and 29 years old I have lived in 7 different cities around the world. Norway and Australia were the two different countries I have made an exchange. Today I live in João Pessoa, a northeast city in Brazil. Environmental engieerning was the subject I chose to study when I went for the Fededral University of Viçosa and I currently hold a bachelor's diploma. Despite the degree, my first job was to work with sales and, at the beginning, I liked it a lot, since I'm a very extrovert person and really easygoing. I've spent 4 years of my life working in this area, until I decided that sales was ok, but not what I really loved doing.

Helping others is where my passion is, and I decided to make a change in my life by helping others with technology and software development. Why not to get together my passion with my hobbies? I started on some basic Python courses, which introduced me to Django. From January 2022 I started to study very hard on this framework, how to deploy using Docker and AWS. Now I'm looking for my first opportunity of working with software or web development.
